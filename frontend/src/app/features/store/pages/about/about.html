The Beginning

It started with a question

Why does the chai at home never taste as good as it should? The leaves are stale, the blends inconsistent, the freshness gone before the packet even opens.

We set out to fix that. Not with fancy packaging or marketing gimmicks — but by going straight to the source. Assam's finest CTC gardens, direct relationships with farmers, and a packing process that seals freshness within hours of processing.

The result? Chai that's honest, bold, and exactly what your morning deserves.
