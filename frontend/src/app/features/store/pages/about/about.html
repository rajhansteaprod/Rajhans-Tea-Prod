About Rajhans Tea Company

Founded in 1970 in Bhopal, Rajhans Tea Company has been a trusted name in premium black tea for over five decades. What started as a small family venture has grown into a renowned brand, known for sourcing the finest Assam CTC leaves and delivering bold, aromatic chai to tea lovers across India.

Our commitment to quality has never wavered. We believe that great black tea should be pure, fresh, and packed with flavor — no shortcuts, no artificial additives. Every batch is carefully selected, processed within 24 hours, and sealed to lock in the perfect taste. For over 50 years, Rajhans has been the choice for those who refuse to compromise on their daily cup of chai.

Our Values
